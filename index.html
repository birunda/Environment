Data Visualization

This is a website that will analyze the World Value Survey dataset on a few certain issues such as Happiness, Environmental Concern, Trust in Government, Religious preference etc. This particular dataset is from the sixth wave survey from years 2010 to 2014.

Tabulations

The survey analysis results are presented in the form of tables such as frequency distributions

Data Visualization

All analysis outputs are presented in a graphical illustration

By Topic of Interest

Both Tabulation and graphical illustrations are combined based on the topic of interest discussed.
